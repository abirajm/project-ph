Q.1 What is Reiki ?

Reiki is a Japanese art of stress reduction and relaxation which also promotes healing. This is simple yet powerful technique of inviting happiness. It holistic approach touches all aspect of life i.e physical,mental, emotional, psychological and spiritual. It comes from 2 Japanese Kanjis i.e Rei-Key; which means Spiritual energy helps non-physical energy in infinite ways. For more details, you can check reiki page. Reiki is discovered by Dr. Mikao Usui in 1922. Usui Mikao ( in Japanese) was the founder of a form of spiritual practice known as Reiki, used as a complementary therapy for the treatment of physical, emotional, and mental diseases. According to the inscription on his memorial stone, Usui taught Reiki to over 2000 people during his lifetime. Sixteen of these students continued their training to reach the Shinpiden level, a level equivalent to the Western third degree, or Master level. Usui died on 9 March 1926 of a stroke.
