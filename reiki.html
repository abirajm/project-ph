Reiki (pronounced "ray-key") is a hands-on Japanese energy healing technique for stress reduction and relaxation . That also promotes healing of mind, body, and spirit. Reiki is based on the understanding that humans and all living things have a universal life force energy that flows through them. Pain and disease result when this energy is low or blocked by physical, emotional, or spiritual distress. Reiki is administered by a gentle "laying on of the hands." Reiki practitioners place their hands on or slightly above a client or from distance and act as a funnel or straw for this life energy. The reiki removes energy blocks and balances the person's energy system. This promotes the body's self-healing ability and provides a sense of peace and well-being.
